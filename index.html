Frequently Asked Questions

How does shift difficulty affect my percentile?

JEE Mains uses normalization. Scoring 180 in a Hard Shift might fetch you 99%ile, while the same score in an Easy Shift might only get you 97%ile due to the higher average performance of students in easier papers.

How is the rank calculated for 2026?

We use the standard formula: ((100 - Percentile) * Total Candidates) / 100. For 2026, we are estimating approximately 14.5 Lakh unique candidates appearing for the exam.

Is this prediction 100% accurate?

No tool can be 100% accurate as the actual results depend on the specific performance of all candidates in your shift. However, our data is based on past year trends to give you the closest possible estimate.
